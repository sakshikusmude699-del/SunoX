Delete Your Account & Data

When you submit a request below, we will verify your identity using the email associated with your account, then permanently delete your SunoX account and the personal data we hold for that account, subject to the retention limits described in our policy section on this page.

Request account deletion

Complete this form to start the process. You must confirm that you understand the action is irreversible.

Use the same email as your SunoX / Google sign-in.

Request received

Your account deletion request has been submitted. You will receive a confirmation email within 24 hours. Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

If your email client opened a draft message to our team, you may send it to help us match your request. Questions? support@soundamplifier.app

What will be deleted

After your request is verified and processed, we remove:

  • Account profile information
  • Usage history
  • Audio recordings and generated content
  • Any stored preferences
  • OAuth tokens and authentication data

Data deletion policy

  • Processing time. We process complete account deletion requests within 30 days of verification, unless a shorter period is required by law.
  • Legal retention. A limited subset of data may be retained for up to 90 days where necessary for legal, regulatory, tax, or security obligations (for example fraud prevention or responding to valid legal process). After that period, such data is deleted or irreversibly anonymized where permitted.
  • Questions. For status updates or concerns about deletion, support@soundamplifier.app
